Website Plan: Stage & Prose

Stage & Prose

Pages & Screens

This name was chosen because it represents the project's duality (books and films) in a simple and memorable way. The "&" symbol suggests the connection between these two art forms.

Website Purpose

The site will be a hub for literature and cinema enthusiasts, offering:

  • Catalog of books and films with detailed information
  • Rating and recommendation system
  • Themed discussion forum
  • Schedule of virtual and in-person meetups
  • Monthly challenges section (e.g., "Read a book and watch its adaptation")

User Scenarios

  1. "I want to find books similar to what I already like" - The site will provide recommendations based on preferences and rating history.
  2. "How can I participate in discussions about the movie of the month?" - The site will have a clear forum section with topics organized by work.
  3. "Are there in-person meetups in my city?" - The calendar will show events filtered by location.
